A Bit About Me

Hey! I’m Shubham Gaur — a tech enthusiast, curious problem-solver, and a self-confessed automation nerd who loves building things that actually work.

By day, I’m a Senior Test Automation Engineer, helping teams ship quality software faster and smarter. By night (and sometimes during coffee breaks), I’m diving into the latest in AI and automation technology — because why not make testing cooler?

Over the last 4+ years, I’ve worked across multiple domains, building solid, scalable automation frameworks using C#, Python, Selenium, Appium, and Playwright. I’ve explored CI/CD pipelines and even brought some AI magic into the QA process.

Beyond the buzzwords, I love learning, experimenting, and improving how we build and test software. I’ve led projects, mentored teammates, and I’m always looking for that next interesting problem to solve.

When I’m not deep in code or tinkering with tools, you’ll find me sketching out new project ideas, or geeking out over the newest trends in tech.
