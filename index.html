A Bit About Me

Hey! I'm Shubham Gaur — a tech enthusiast, curious problem-solver, and a self-confessed ML nerd who loves building things that actually work in the real world.

By day, I'm a Senior Engineer building ML-powered systems that solve real business problems — from multilingual evaluation pipelines to RAG systems for private documents. By night (and sometimes during coffee breaks), I'm diving deeper into AI research, exploring context-aware systems, and experimenting with model evaluation techniques — because making ML production-ready is where the real fun begins.
